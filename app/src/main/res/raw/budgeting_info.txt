Many individuals have a hard time managing their finances. But not to worry! This app will record the time and day of whatever you budgeted as well as your losses and gains!

This app provides the user an option that can give the user full control over recording their finances, and then uploading to an online private database that we implement via Firebase! This gives the user control over real-time recorded finances and allows them to keep track so you no longer need to worry about forgetting how much you lost or gained!
